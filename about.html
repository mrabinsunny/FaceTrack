About Us

Welcome to FaceTrack, your premier solution for efficient and reliable attendance management!

At FaceTrack, we pride ourselves on being pioneers in the realm of group facial-recognition based attendance systems. Developed by a dedicated team of experts including Abin Sunny, Abid Muhammed, Aiswarya Lakshmi, and Ancita J Feriah, FaceTrack offers cutting-edge technology designed to streamline attendance processes for educational institutions, businesses, and organizations of all sizes.

Our platform boasts two intuitive modes for attendance tracking. The first mode harnesses the power of facial recognition technology, allowing for the simultaneous detection of multiple faces and seamless attendance capture. With FaceTrack, time-consuming manual processes are a thing of the past as our system efficiently records attendance with unparalleled accuracy.

For scenarios where facial recognition may not be suitable, FaceTrack offers a convenient manual attendance option. Teachers and administrators can effortlessly take attendance with just a few clicks, ensuring that every individual's presence is accurately logged.

Furthermore, FaceTrack simplifies the process of onboarding new students or members. Our user-friendly interface allows for swift registration by simply capturing a picture of the individual, eliminating the hassle of tedious data entry.

At FaceTrack, we are committed to providing reliable, secure, and user-friendly attendance solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Whether you're managing a classroom, overseeing a corporate event, or monitoring attendance in any other setting, FaceTrack is your trusted partner for seamless attendance management.
